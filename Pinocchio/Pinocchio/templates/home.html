About Pinocchio

Pinocchio is a website for students to register for their college courses. The process is simple. Your college admin will create all the courses and then create the registration form. The registration starts on a particular date and time and stays live till the registration end time. You can go ahead and signup yourself to register for the courses. To see upcoming or live registrations click Registrations on the top.
